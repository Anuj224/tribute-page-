General Bipin Rawat

1958 - 2021

First Chief of Defence Staff of India

Legacy of Service

General Bipin Rawat served as the first Chief of Defence Staff of India, the highest-ranking military officer in the Indian Armed Forces. His distinguished career spanned over four decades, during which he served with honor and dedication to the nation.

Military Career Highlights

  • Commissioned into the 5th battalion of 11 Gorkha Rifles in 1978
  • Commanded an infantry battalion along the Line of Actual Control
  • Appointed as Chief of Army Staff in 2016
  • Became India's first Chief of Defence Staff in 2020
  • Awarded with numerous honors including PVSM, UYSM, AVSM, YSM, SM, VSM

Vision and Leadership

General Rawat was instrumental in modernizing the Indian military and implementing crucial reforms. He worked tirelessly towards the integration of the three services - Army, Navy, and Air Force.

"The military should be far away from politics. The military should be ready to handle any challenge."
